  Injected+Manifested coincide at the first observing read (clock.read

  reads honestly leaves the skew Armed. fault.skipped arm REMOVED.

  inject/manifest semantics stated).
- tests/runtime.rs: 3 new adversarial tests —
  clock_skew_manifests_on_the_first_observing_read (ladder + exact

  valid completion), clock_skew_accumulates_and_replays_bit_identically
