## Proposal in one paragraph

After the post-audit truth substrate is complete, build a bounded evolution lab
that improves how `vibe-halt` searches its multiverse. The proposer evolves
strictly typed, non-executable universe genomes—fault weights, bounded schedule
parameters, and allowlisted scenario values—while a frozen evaluator measures
them and an independently authored verifier reruns finalists from clean current
`main`. The lab operates offline under a fixed deterministic budget, preserves
every candidate and loser lineage in durable evidence, and ends with either one
opt-in champion proposal or an honest null/kill. It never evolves Rust, Python,
shell, gates, oracles, evidence schemas, containment, or its own fitness rule,
and it never promotes its own result.

## Why this phase should exist

The post-audit controller is constructing the truth substrate: complete
observation identity, fail-closed oracles, exact recall, strict v2 evidence,
truthful subprocess outcomes, child-visible cassette transport, verifier parity,
and measured D2 reach. Once that substrate is human-merged and independently
proven, the next multiplier is controlled search over better test universes—not
another grand architecture, dashboard, or self-modifying agent framework.

Evolution is useful only if the search cannot rewrite the rules by which it is
judged. This phase therefore defends against:

- Goodharting a visible recall count or saturated bug;
- editing evaluator, corpus split, denominator, seeds, or budget after seeing a
  result;
- rewarding crashes, timeouts, invalid inputs, output volume, or fingerprint
  proliferation;
- calling repeated success on known canaries “novelty”;
- presenting the proposer’s own measurement as independent proof;
- evolving executable payloads before there is a containment boundary;
- and losing multi-hour work because only a SHA—not its Git objects or evidence—
  survived outside transient scratch.

## 1. Honest meaning of self-evolution

The first forge version evolves only canonical, inert policy genomes.

### 1.1 Allowed genome surface

A genome may contain only strictly parsed, bounded values already meaningful to
the deterministic runner, such as:

- typed fault-family masks and integer weights;
- bounded schedule-policy parameters for already admitted policies;
- allowlisted scenario parameters and typed workload knobs;
- mutation-operator weights; and
- proposer heuristics encoded as versioned data.

Every field has explicit type, range, order, default, and canonical encoding.
Unknown fields, duplicate fields, noncanonical order, oversized values,
conditional/introspection operators, paths, and arbitrary bytes interpreted as
code are rejected fail-closed.

An exact decision tape is evidence for one execution, not automatically a
general policy improvement. A genome must describe a reproducible policy whose
behavior can be evaluated over predeclared dispersed universes.

A genome cannot influence shrink inputs, hints, algorithm, or budget. Shrink
preservation is evaluator output only.

### 1.2 Forbidden mutation surface

The evolutionary loop must never generate, modify, supply, or execute:

- Rust, Python, shell, build logic, manifests, dependencies, CI, or gates;
- controllers, governance, evaluator, oracle, observation, trace, replay,
  cassette, evidence, or shrink logic;
- fitness computation, denominators, controls, holdout generator, seeds,
  budgets, limits, allowlists, or promotion policy;
- filesystem paths, network destinations, syscalls, FFI, unsafe, helpers,
  supervisor behavior, credentials, or executable payloads; or
- corpus truth, baselines, canaries, receipt schemas, or frozen identities.

A code-level improvement discovered during the experiment becomes an ordinary,
bounded, human-reviewed draft-PR proposal outside the evolutionary loop. It is
not scored as a genome and receives no automatic compilation, execution, or
promotion authority from this controller.

### 1.3 Three immutable planes

1. **Proposer plane:** creates candidate genomes and ancestry under the frozen
   mutation contract.
2. **Evaluator plane:** measures the frozen candidates under an exact evaluator
   trusted-computing-base (TCB) digest.
3. **Verifier plane:** independently re-derives and reruns finalists from clean
   current main with no proposer cache or adaptive feedback.

No plane may modify another during an epoch. The proposer’s measurement is
discovery evidence, never promotion proof.

## 2. Standing law

1. **Human promotion only.** Never merge, approve, self-approve, mark ready,
   enable auto-merge, dismiss review, or push to `main`.
2. **Frozen evaluator TCB.** Before every epoch, record source SHA/tree and the
   exact blob/digest set for evaluator, oracle, corpus split, gates, limits,
   evidence schemas, and selection logic. Any dirty file or mismatch aborts.
3. **Candidate immutability.** Once canonical finalist identities are frozen,
   no candidate, ancestry, evaluator, denominator, seed rule, limit, or metric
   may change. A change starts a new non-comparable epoch.
4. **No test weakening or work reduction.** Deleting a regression, widening a
   tolerance, lowering a budget, omitting outcomes, repinning expected values,
   or doing less work cannot produce improvement.
5. **Every outcome remains in the denominator.** Invalid cases, timeouts,
   infrastructure failures, `UNCHECKED`, and divergence are never silently
   discarded.
6. **Machine evidence outranks model agreement.** Multiple agents can propose
   or review, but no model vote is verification and no LLM is a judge.
7. **Hard floors before ranking.** A safety, control, replay, identity,
   validity, or evidence failure disqualifies; it cannot be offset by yield,
   novelty, speed, or smaller size.
8. **No scalar fitness laundering.** Use lexicographic/Pareto comparison with
   raw counts and paired evidence. Never hide tradeoffs in one weighted score.
9. **Known canaries earn zero novelty.** They prove harness health. Novel
   failures remain quarantined and unscored until independent reproduction and
   human root-cause classification.
10. **No hidden authority.** No unsafe/helper, new repository/dependency,
    public target, live provider, credential, network, spending, or external
    action.
11. **No architecture sprawl.** Reuse multiverse, observation, trace, v2
    evidence, replay, shrink, corpus, verifier, and CI. No new daemon, database,
    queue, dashboard, plugin system, policy engine, distributed fleet, second
    receipt/identity scheme, embedded LLM, or optimization-framework zoo.
12. **One owner per surface.** One coordinator, one integration writer per
    shared surface, exact writable manifests, clean worktrees, governance WIP.
13. **Durability before the long run.** Code branches are remotely durable
    before execution. Every generation’s complete population, losers, ancestry,
    seeds, budgets, evaluator digest, v2 bundle references, and checkpoint
    digest are persisted outside transient scratch with retention recorded.
14. **Truthful nulls.** `NO_WINNER` is a legitimate successful outcome. Never
    expand budget, alter seeds, reveal holdout feedback, or move the metric
    after seeing a null.

## 8. E2V — independent evaluator and ranking contract

The verifier owner independently derives evaluator results from canonical
inputs. Freeze its TCB digest before each epoch.

### 8.1 Hard admissibility floors

A genome is eligible only if:

- root gates, frozen identities, controls, and exact-count contracts remain
  green;
- false `CLEAN`, `DIVERGENT`, and `UNCHECKED` counts do not regress;
- all outcomes remain in the denominator;
- every claimed finding replays byte-for-byte at least 3/3 under the same
  universe;
- invalid, timeout, resource, and infrastructure outcomes are typed and cannot
  earn fitness;
- candidate ancestry and v2 evidence are complete; and
- checkpoint/resume is deterministic.

The 3/3 replay floor is discovery eligibility only. Every finding relied on by
`OPT_IN_CHAMPION` must meet 100/100 replay or the stricter current-main
predecessor contract.

### 8.2 Ranking eligible genomes

Compare by lexicographic/Pareto evidence, not one scalar:

1. independently confirmed breadth across predeclared unsaturated classes;
2. exact held-out recall with no control or existing-corpus regression;
3. paired censored universes-to-first-finding against baseline and uniform;
4. shrink preservation and exact replay/actionability;
5. valid behavioral reach under equal work; and
6. event-normalized resource cost last.

Raw crash count, timeout count, output volume, wall time, invalid-input count,
superficial coverage, fingerprint count, and known canaries earn no fitness.
Deduplicate by complete canonical observation plus independently classified root
cause—not log text or a digest alone. Unconfirmed “novel” failures are
quarantined, not scored.

**Acceptance:** the independent evaluator reproduces baseline and candidate
results from frozen artifacts and cannot be influenced by genome fields.

**Kill:** evaluator TCB drift, denominator mismatch, identity disagreement,
control regression, or different output for identical canonical input.

## 11. E5 — post-freeze holdout, independent replay, and ablation

2. generates the fresh holdout manifest under E2K law;
3. runs baseline, uniform policy, and each finalist on identical paired holdout

second disjoint post-freeze holdout epoch.

the champion/null under the same frozen artifacts.

**Kill:** holdout generated before finalist freeze, holdout leakage, adaptive
feedback, independent result mismatch, train-only improvement, or improvement

- proposer, evaluator, and verifier TCBs are separated and content-bound;
- challenge suite is depth>=2, unsaturated, fail-closed, and control-backed;
- every outcome stays in the denominator and hard floors precede ranking;
- population, loser ancestry, checkpoint/resume, and v2 evidence are complete
  and deterministic;
- durable recovery is exercised before the long run; and
- no default, target, unsafe, dependency, or promotion authority leaked in.

### 16.2 Full phase outcome

Calibration, one frozen training epoch, and one independent post-freeze holdout
finish at a fixed budget without moving metrics. Exactly one is true:

1. `OPT_IN_CHAMPION`: one genome independently clears all floors and the
   predeclared holdout comparison; it is published only as opt-in and remains a
   human decision;
2. `NO_WINNER`: the exact null and complete population evidence are published,
   and autonomous search stops; or
3. `EVOLUTION_LAB_KILLED`: the violated boundary is identified, experiment
   machinery is isolated/reverted as appropriate, and ordinary development
   continues.

The phase is not complete because agents generated many candidates, one score
rose, CI once passed, or models agreed. Code completion lives on current merged
main; experiment completion lives in immutable, independently replayed evidence.

## 17. Operator packet

At a genuine human gate, report only:

- current main SHA/tree and merged-main battery;
- predecessor package and safe-phase status;
- E0-E6 package/epoch status;
- exact evaluator TCB, genome schema, baseline, candidates, finalists, nonce,
  train/holdout manifests, budgets, and raw denominators;
- artifact retention/recovery proof;
- CI jobs/steps, reviews, and unresolved threads;
- terminal outcome, remaining claim boundary, and kill disposition; and
- one requested human action.

Never ask the operator to choose among unqualified genomes. Never recommend a
default flip from the first holdout. Never present a proposer result as
independent verification.

## Essence distilled

```text
finish truth substrate
    -> human-admit inert-genome evolution
    -> freeze proposer/evaluator/verifier planes
    -> calibrate without promotion
    -> evolve under fixed training budget
    -> freeze finalists and terminate proposer
    -> generate fresh holdout
    -> independently replay and ablate
    -> opt-in champion OR honest null/kill
```

`vibe-halt` becomes able to evolve how it searches without gaining permission to
rewrite the machine, the judge, or the rules of promotion. That separation is
the heart of the phase.
